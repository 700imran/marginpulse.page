Capabilities

Built for Indian finance teams.

Every feature designed around how Indian businesses actually work — not adapted from foreign software.

🔄

Real-Time Bank Sync

Connects to your bank via RBI's Account Aggregator framework. Every debit and credit visible the moment it posts. Never export a CSV again.

🧾

AI Invoice Matching

Sales and purchase invoices matched against bank transactions using our Levenshtein + value + date matching engine. Confidence-scored. Tunable tolerances.

⚡

Live ITC Monitoring

Tracks every vendor's GSTR-1 filing status in real time. ITC risk surfaces before the deadline — not in the next month's GSTR-2B.

💰

Revenue Leakage Detection

Payments made without invoices. Invoices raised without payments. Found instantly — not during a year-end audit when it's too late to act.

🔍

Smart Anomaly Alerts

Value variances, date drifts, duplicate payment risks, and GST mismatches — severity-scored and sent to you with suggested actions, not just a flag.

📊

Audit-Ready Reports

Every transaction traceable end-to-end from bank entry to invoice to GST record. One-click export at any time. No scrambling before filing deadlines.

💬

WhatsApp Document Ingest

Forward any invoice or receipt to your MarginPulse WhatsApp number — OCR processes and queues it for reconciliation automatically.

✉

Vendor Reminder Automation

AI-drafted follow-up messages to vendors whose late GST filings are blocking your ITC. Send via WhatsApp or email in one click.

👥

Team Access & Roles

Add your CA, accountant, or finance team members with role-based access. Every action logged for security and accountability.
