About MarginPulse Pro

Built because reconciliation was broken — and expensive.

We're a team of ex-finance professionals and engineers who got tired of watching Indian businesses lose money to mismatches they discovered too late to fix. So we built the system we always wished existed.

The ₹24,000 crore problem nobody talks about.

Indian mid-size businesses collectively lose thousands of crores every year to unclaimed ITC, undetected revenue leakage, and manual reconciliation errors. Not because they're careless — but because the tools available to them work with yesterday's data, not today's.

Bank statements get exported once a month. GSTR-2B is downloaded after the fact. Invoices pile up in email. Everything is reconciled in Excel, after month-end, when most issues are already past their recovery window.

MarginPulse Pro is our answer to that: direct API connections to your bank and the GST portal, running continuously, so every mismatch is caught while it's still recoverable — not reported in a PDF after it's already a write-off.

Security & Trust

Your financial data deserves the same protection as your bank.

MarginPulse Pro is built on India's own regulated financial data infrastructure. Here's exactly how we protect your data — no vague promises.

🔒

RBI Account Aggregator Framework

Bank connections use India's regulatory AA framework — a consent-based, read-only mechanism approved by RBI. We never see your bank credentials. You authorize directly with your bank. You revoke access directly from your bank app.

🛡️

Read-Only Access, Always

MarginPulse Pro can read your transaction data to reconcile it. It cannot initiate transfers, make payments, update account details, or take any action on your behalf — by design, not by policy.

🔐

Bank-Grade Encryption

All data is encrypted in transit using TLS 1.3 and at rest using AES-256. Your financial data is never stored in plaintext at any point in our infrastructure.

📋

GSP/GSTN API Integration

GST portal access is via the official Government-authorised GSP channel. We access exactly the data you authorise — GSTR-2A/2B filings — and nothing else from the portal.

🚫

Zero Third-Party Data Sharing

Your financial data is never sold, shared with advertisers, or used to train AI models. It is used exclusively to provide you with reconciliation results.

✅

Revoke Access Anytime

You can revoke MarginPulse Pro's access to your bank account at any time directly from your bank's mobile app — no need to contact us. GST portal access can be revoked from the portal settings.

🏗️

Infrastructure Security

Hosted on secure cloud infrastructure with daily backups, access controls, and audit logs. Our internal team access to customer data is role-gated and logged at every level.

📧

Responsible Disclosure

Found a security issue? We operate a responsible disclosure programme. Contact security@marginpulsepro.in and we'll respond within 24 hours with a remediation timeline.

Questions about our security posture?

We're happy to walk through our architecture, data flows, and certifications with your security or legal team.

1. Who We Are

MarginPulse Pro is operated by MarginPulse Technologies Private Limited ("we", "us", "our"), incorporated under the Companies Act, 2013 (CIN: U72900KA2026PTC000001), registered in Bangalore, Karnataka, India.

We operate the platform at app.marginpulsepro.in and the marketing website at marginpulsepro.in (collectively, the "Service").

2. What Data We Collect

Account Information

When you register, we collect your business name, email address, GSTIN, phone number, and a hashed version of your password. We never store your password in plaintext.

Financial Data

With your explicit authorisation, we access and process your bank transaction data (via RBI Account Aggregator), your invoice data (uploaded or synced), and your GST filing data (via GSP/GSTN APIs). This data is used exclusively to provide the reconciliation service.

Usage Data

We collect standard server logs (IP address, browser type, pages visited, timestamps) and in-app usage patterns to improve the product. This data is anonymised for analytics purposes.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide, operate, and improve the reconciliation service
  • To send you reconciliation alerts, anomaly notifications, and product updates
  • To respond to your support queries
  • To comply with applicable Indian law and regulatory requirements
  • To detect and prevent fraud or abuse of the platform

We do not use your financial data for advertising, marketing to third parties, or training AI models.

4. Bank & GST Portal Data

Bank transaction data is accessed via the RBI-regulated Account Aggregator (AA) framework under your explicit, revocable consent. Access is read-only. We cannot initiate transactions.

GST portal data is accessed via Government-authorised GSP/GSTN APIs under your explicit authorisation. We access only the filing data required for reconciliation (GSTR-2A, GSTR-2B) and no other data from the portal.

Both authorisations can be revoked at any time — bank access from your bank's mobile app, GST access from the portal settings — without contacting us.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We share data only with:

  • Infrastructure providers: Cloud hosting and database providers operating under data processing agreements with equivalent security standards.
  • Legal requirements: We will disclose data if required by a court order, SEBI or GST authorities, or other legally valid process under Indian law.

6. Data Retention

Your account data is retained for the duration of your subscription plus 90 days after termination to enable recovery of your data if needed. Financial transaction data processed for reconciliation is retained for 7 years in compliance with Indian accounting regulations. You may request earlier deletion of non-regulated data by contacting us.

7. Your Rights

Under applicable Indian privacy law, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact privacy@marginpulsepro.in. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Security

We use TLS 1.3 for data in transit and AES-256 encryption for data at rest. Our infrastructure is hosted in India. Internal access to customer data is role-based and logged. We conduct security reviews periodically and operate a responsible disclosure programme for security researchers.

9. Cookies

We use strictly necessary cookies (for authentication and session management) and, with your consent, analytics cookies. See our Cookie Policy for details.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences, keep you logged in, and understand how the site is used.

These cookies are required for the Service to function and cannot be switched off. They are set in response to your actions such as logging in or setting your cookie preferences.

  • mp_refresh_token — Used to refresh your session without requiring re-login. Duration: 30 days.
  • mp_tenant_id — Identifies your business account. Required for the app to load your data. Duration: 30 days.
  • cookie_consent — Records your cookie preference. Duration: 1 year.

Analytics Cookies (With Consent)

These cookies help us understand how visitors use the marketing website so we can improve it. All analytics data is anonymised.
