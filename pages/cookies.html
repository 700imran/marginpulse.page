Cookie Policy

Last updated: 1 June 2026

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences, keep you logged in, and understand how the site is used.

Cookies We Use

Strictly Necessary Cookies (Always Active)

Required for the Service to function and cannot be switched off. Set in response to your actions such as logging in or setting cookie preferences.

  • mp_access_token — Your authentication session token. Required to stay logged in. Duration: 24 hours.
  • mp_refresh_token — Used to refresh your session without requiring re-login. Duration: 30 days.
  • mp_tenant_id — Identifies your business account. Required for the app to load your data. Duration: 30 days.
  • mp_cookie_consent — Records your cookie preference. Duration: 1 year.

Analytics Cookies (With Consent)

Help us understand how visitors use the marketing website so we can improve it. All analytics data is anonymised.

  • _mp_analytics — Anonymous session identifier for page-view analytics. Duration: 30 days.

Cookies We Do Not Use

We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. We do not share cookie data with advertisers. We do not use third-party tracking pixels.

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling strictly necessary cookies will prevent the app from working correctly. You can update your analytics cookie preference at any time using the banner shown on first visit.
