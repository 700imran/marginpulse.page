Security & Trust

Your financial data deserves the same protection as your bank.

MarginPulse Pro is built on India's own regulated financial data infrastructure. Here's exactly how we protect your data — no vague promises.

🔒

RBI Account Aggregator Framework

Bank connections use India's regulatory AA framework — a consent-based, read-only mechanism approved by RBI. We never see your credentials. You authorize directly with your bank. Revoke access anytime from your bank app.

🛡️

Read-Only Access, Always

MarginPulse Pro can read your transactions to reconcile them. It cannot initiate transfers, make payments, or take any action on your behalf — by design, not policy. This is a cryptographic constraint, not a setting.

🔐

AES-256 Encryption

All data is encrypted in transit using TLS 1.3 and at rest using AES-256. Bank account numbers are field-encrypted in the database using Fernet symmetric encryption — never stored in plaintext, even internally.

📋

GSP/GSTN API Integration

GST portal access is via the official Government-authorised GSP channel. We access exactly the data you authorise — GSTR-2A/2B filings — nothing else from the portal.

🚫

Zero Third-Party Data Sharing

Your financial data is never sold, shared with advertisers, or used to train AI models. It is used exclusively to provide you with reconciliation outputs.

✅

Revoke Access Anytime

Bank access revocable directly from your bank's mobile app. GST portal access revocable from portal settings. No need to contact us. Revocation is immediate.

⚡

Rate Limiting & DoS Protection

All API endpoints are rate-limited. Login is capped at 5 requests/minute per IP. Uploads have a 25MB hard limit enforced before files are read into memory. Constant-time password verification prevents timing attacks.

📧

Responsible Disclosure

Found a security issue? Email security@marginpulsepro.in. We respond within 24 hours with a remediation timeline and will credit your disclosure publicly.
