About MarginPulse Pro

Built because reconciliation was broken — and expensive.

We're a team of ex-finance professionals and engineers who got tired of watching Indian businesses lose money to mismatches they discovered too late to fix. So we built the system we always wished existed.

Our Mission

The ₹24,000 crore problem nobody talks about.

Indian mid-size businesses collectively lose thousands of crores every year to unclaimed ITC, undetected revenue leakage, and manual reconciliation errors — not because they're careless, but because the tools available to them work with yesterday's data, not today's.

Bank statements get exported once a month. GSTR-2B is downloaded after the fact. Invoices pile up in email. Everything gets reconciled in Excel, after month-end, when most issues are already past their recovery window.

MarginPulse Pro is our answer: direct API connections to your bank and the GST portal, running continuously, so every mismatch is caught while it's still recoverable — not reported in a PDF after it's already a write-off.

Our Core Belief
❌

A reconciliation report that tells you what you already lost is a verdict — accurate, but useless.

✓

A real-time alert that tells you what you're about to lose — with enough time to act — is a save.

Our Values

How we work.

Accuracy above all

We're handling your money. There's no tolerance for close-enough. Every rule in our matching engine is tested against real GST guidelines.

Real-time or nothing

Data that's a day old is already behind. We built for live connections from day one — not as a feature, as the product's core premise.

Recovery, not reporting

The goal is never to tell you what you lost. It's to alert you early enough that you don't lose it. Every alert is timed to be actionable.

Indian compliance first

Built on Indian financial infrastructure — Account Aggregator, GSP/GSTN. This is native, not adapted from foreign software.
